Save all your inspiration

Inspiration comes in many ways and you like to save everything for the internet. Collect.io allow you to keep all of your inspiration at the same place from pictures to links, texts and colors.

Picture

Save your favorites pictures from all over the web. It could be static jpegs, pngs or animated assets like gif and video.

Note

Write your next article ideas, create a todo list or just take some note. Our note system use markdown to create powerful document.

Link

Keep url of your last favorite website.

Color Palette

Add the last pantone of the year or just create a new palette that you just find.

Keep it clean

Organise your resources

Use collection to group your inspiration and tag your files to get the place always nice and tidy. Always find what you are looking for.

Take control over your interface

UWe wanted our users to have a total contol on the interface. You will be either able to choose where you want to put Collect and what storage you want to use.

Interface

We let you decide how autonomous you want to be, you can directly log in here and use our interface, or use your own domain name.

  • Personal domain
  • Localhost
Storage

Create an account and plug Collect.io to your storage solution, you will have a 50mb limitation if you don't use any cloud service.

  • Dropbox
  • FTP
  • Amazon S3
